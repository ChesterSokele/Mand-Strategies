About Manda Strategies

At Manda Strategies, we are committed to shaping inclusive, resilient, and globally connected communities through innovative consulting and strategic partnerships. We specialize in subnational diplomacy, international engagement, urban development, and equity-centered public policy, bringing over a decade of experience grounded in real-world impact.

Our team collaborates with governments, nonprofits, and private sector organizations to design and deliver solutions that advance equity, strengthen communities, and drive sustainable growth. By bridging global perspectives with local needs, we help our clients build meaningful partnerships, navigate complex challenges, and create lasting change.
